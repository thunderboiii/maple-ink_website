    How do we work?

    Get to know our workflow and together we'll tell your story.

    1

    Before we begin to make any concepts, we want to understand you, your brand and its values.

    2

    Together we will write your story and from that create a corporate identity. Laying the foundation for color palettes, style guides and more.

    3

    In order to secure consistency throughout the story that is your brand, we create style guides that contain important aspects such as colors, icons, fonts and more representing your brand.

    4

    Then finally, we fabricate unique and thorough advertising material making your brand recognizable in an instant

    Why Brand Design by Maple Ink?

    Consistency

    We provide you with professional style guides that secure the consistency of your brand.

    Recognition

    Professional Brand Design helps people recognize your brand faster.

    Simplicity

    Our process is optimized so that you can just lay back and tell us about your brand. We do the rest.
